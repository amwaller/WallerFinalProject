The Unequal Burden: Married Women Bear the Brunt of Poverty Among New Mothers

The 2021 U.S. Census Data reveals that married women who gave birth in the last 12 months are more likely to be living in poverty than unmarried women. These findings highlight the economic challenges faced by new mothers in the United States.

By: Alisha Waller
